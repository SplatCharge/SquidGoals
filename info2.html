Register Discord Channel:

Registering your Discord Channel will allow you and your discord group to upload images directly to a discord channel..
To Register:
  1. You must have admin privileges for the channel
  2. Log into Discord
  3. Click on the edit Channel Gear next to the channel you want to add it to
  4. Click on Webhooks
  5. Create Webhook
  6. Give it a unique name, this will act as your teams passcode.
  7. Copy the Webhook URL and paste it below

* Never really messed with Discord before this. This seemed like the simplest way to set it up. It just seemed easy, and youcan always just delete your webhook and remake it, if a problem occurs. However, if you know a better way to set this up let me know.

Some things to keep in mind:

  • Changing the map or mode will clear the drawing
  • To Manipulate an object first click on it after it is created
  • You can pinch zoom now and the nav at the bottom should survive.
  • You can remove the background and border from objects by making them transparent.
  • it should defualt to vertical on phones and horzontal on computers but you can change it manually in the maps/mode area. You will lose you drawing though.
